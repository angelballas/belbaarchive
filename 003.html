BELBA ARCHIVE
ATHENS
ENTRY 003
BEFORE AND AFTER SUNSET
2025
Two pieces structured around a single temporal threshold.

BEFORE SUNSET holds the last sustained warmth of the day. Elements remain suspended, illuminated but already receding.

AFTER SUNSET follows the transition. Tone shifts toward lower contrast. Space opens. Movement slows without fully stopping.

Both tracks were constructed on the Roland SP-606 using sampled material preserved with minimal intervention. Texture, repetition, and tonal balance determine progression rather than melodic development.

Recorded in Athens.
