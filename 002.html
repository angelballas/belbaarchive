ENTRY 002
LOST IN FLORIDA
2024
Lost in Florida brings together elements of hip-hop and lo-fi through a restrained and deliberate use of sampling. Built around fragments reorganized on the Roland SP-606, the material combines harmonic loops, vocal traces, and sustained tonal layers into a continuous listening field.

Each track maintains its own internal balance while contributing to the overall pacing of the release. The pieces unfold without emphasis or resolution, allowing repetition, texture, and spatial continuity to define the structure.

Produced in Athens, Greece.
