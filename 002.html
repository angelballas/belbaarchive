BELBA ARCHIVE
ATHENS
ENTRY 002
LOST IN FLORIDA
2024
A collection of instrumental studies organized around tonal drift and surface texture.

The material combines sampled harmonic fragments, processed vocal traces, and sustained tonal elements arranged in slow, cyclic structures.

Rhythmic movement remains restrained. Emphasis is placed on atmosphere, internal balance, and gradual transformation rather than linear development.

The work maintains a consistent spatial character throughout, allowing individual pieces to function both independently and as parts of a continuous environment.

Recorded in Athens.
