BELBA ARCHIVE

FOR THE FAMILY / ASW
2022
Two instrumental works constructed as a paired structure, examining contrast, repetition, and spatial continuity.

The material combines digitally sequenced elements with performed instrumentation, establishing an early structural framework centered on tonal balance and sustained form.

Produced in Athens, Greece.
